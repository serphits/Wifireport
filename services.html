Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free tier really free?

Yes, 100% free — no credit card required, no hidden fees. Our free tier includes real-time WiFi security scanning, speed tests, privacy checks, and more. Use it as often as you like, forever.

What's included in Pro PDF reports?

Pro PDF reports include comprehensive network analysis covering security posture, speed performance history, privacy audit results, advanced recommendations, and monthly health scores — all in a shareable, professionally formatted document.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Yes, absolutely. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time and retain access through the end of your billing period.

How does Business pricing work?

Business pricing is customized based on the number of sites you need to monitor and the number of team members who need access. Contact our sales team for a tailored quote.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for non-profit organizations and educational institutions. Contact us to learn more about available discounts.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All free-tier analysis is performed 100% client-side in your browser — no data ever leaves your device. Our paid infrastructure is SOC 2 compliant, with end-to-end encryption and strict access controls.

Our team is happy to walk you through the options and recommend the best fit for your needs.
