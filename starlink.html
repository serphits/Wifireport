Frequently Asked Questions

How many Starlink satellites are in orbit?

As of January 2026, there are over 9,400 Starlink satellites in active orbit, forming the world's largest satellite constellation. SpaceX continues to launch new satellites regularly to expand coverage and capacity globally.

Is Starlink faster than fiber?

Starlink V3 offers speeds up to 300+ Mbps with median latency around 20ms, making it competitive with many fiber connections. While fiber may offer higher peak speeds in urban areas, Starlink excels in rural and remote locations where fiber infrastructure is unavailable. The low latency makes Starlink suitable for gaming, video calls, and streaming.

When is Starlink available in South Africa?

Starlink became available in South Africa in 2023 and continues to expand coverage across the country in 2026. Service is available in most regions with active pre-orders being fulfilled. Check the official Starlink website for real-time availability at your specific address.
