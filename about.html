About WiFi.Report

We're on a mission to help everyone understand and optimize their WiFi networks with powerful, privacy-focused analysis tools.

Our Story

WiFi.Report was born from a simple observation: most people have no idea how their WiFi network is performing or whether it's secure. We created this tool to give everyday users access to powerful network analysis.

Our Mission

At WiFi.Report, we believe everyone deserves to know how their network is performing and whether their data is secure. Our mission is to educate, protect privacy, simplify complexity, and empower action.

What Makes Us Different

  • Privacy First - No data collection, everything happens in your browser.
  • No Installation Required - Access powerful analysis instantly from any browser.
  • Real Results - Using actual browser APIs and real network measurements.
  • User-Friendly Design - Complex metrics transformed into intuitive visualizations.
  • Actionable Recommendations - Every analysis includes specific improvement steps.
  • Free and Accessible - No hidden fees or premium paywalls.
