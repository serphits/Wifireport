About WiFi.Report

We're on a mission to help everyone understand and optimize their WiFi networks with powerful, privacy-focused analysis tools.

Our Story

WiFi.Report was built to solve a real problem: most people have no reliable way to check whether their home network is secure, leaking their real IP address, or performing as it should. We created this tool to give everyone access to professional-grade network analysis — free, instant, and without installing anything.

Our tools are used by network administrators, privacy researchers, remote workers, travellers, and everyday users who want confidence their connection is private and performing well. Every test runs entirely in your browser — we have no server-side visibility into your network data.

Our Expertise

WiFi.Report covers the full stack of home and business network security:

  • WiFi Security Analysis — HTTPS detection, TLS inspection, HSTS verification, and connection exposure testing
  • Privacy & VPN Testing — IP leak detection, DNS leak testing, WebRTC vulnerability scanning
  • Internet Speed Measurement — Real-time download, upload, latency, and jitter testing
  • Connection Stability Monitoring — Packet loss and consistency analysis
  • Educational Resources — 30+ expert guides covering WiFi security, router configuration, VPN selection, and network optimisation

Our Mission

WiFi security shouldn't require expensive consultants or complex software. Our mission is to democratise network security analysis — giving every user, from students to IT professionals, instant access to the same diagnostic information security experts use.

Why WiFi.Report Is Different

  • 100% Client-Side Processing — Every analysis runs in your browser. We never see your test results, IP address, or network data.
  • No Installation Required — Open the page, click once, get results in 30 seconds. No extensions, no apps, no accounts.
  • Real Measurements — We use actual browser APIs and genuine network requests, not estimated results.
  • Actionable Results — Every test result comes with specific, plain-English recommendations.
  • Always Free — No freemium upsells, no paywalled features, no premium tier.
  • Expert-Written Guides — Our blog covers 30+ topics from WPA3 encryption to DNS leaks, written for clarity and accuracy.

Transparency & Trust

We believe trust is earned through transparency. Here's exactly what happens when you use WiFi.Report:

  • What we test: Your browser makes standard HTTPS requests to measure speed, checks for WebRTC IP exposure using browser APIs, and analyses your connection's security headers — all standard techniques used by security professionals.
  • What we don't do: We do not store your IP address, test results, or any personally identifiable information on our servers.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics for aggregate page view statistics only (no individual user tracking of test results). You can opt out via browser privacy settings.
  • Advertising: We display Google AdSense ads to fund the free service. Ads are clearly labelled and do not influence our recommendations.
