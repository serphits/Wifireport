About WiFi.Report

We're on a mission to help everyone understand and optimize their WiFi networks with powerful, privacy-focused analysis tools.

Our Story

WiFi.Report was born from a simple observation: most people have no idea how their WiFi network is performing or whether it's secure. While advanced users have access to complex network diagnostic tools, everyday users are left in the dark about their connection quality, security vulnerabilities, and privacy concerns.

We set out to change that by creating a tool that's powerful enough for tech enthusiasts but simple enough for anyone to use. Our goal is to democratize network analysis and empower users with the knowledge they need to make informed decisions about their internet connection.

Our Mission

At WiFi.Report, we believe that everyone deserves to know how their network is performing and whether their data is secure. Our mission is to:

  • Educate Users Provide clear, actionable insights about network performance and security in language anyone can understand.
  • Protect Privacy Build tools that respect user privacy by performing all analysis locally in the browser窶馬o data collection, no tracking.
  • Simplify Complexity Transform complex network metrics into easy-to-understand visualizations and recommendations.
  • Empower Action Give users practical steps they can take to improve their network security, speed, and stability.

What Makes Us Different

WiFi.Report stands apart from other network tools in several key ways:

  • Privacy First Unlike many online tools, we don't collect, store, or transmit your network data. Everything happens in your browser.
  • No Installation Required Access powerful network analysis instantly from any browser窶馬o downloads, no apps, no hassle.
  • Real Results, Not Simulations We use actual browser APIs and real network measurements to provide accurate, actionable insights.
  • User-Friendly Design Complex network metrics are transformed into intuitive visualizations with clear explanations.
  • Actionable Recommendations Every analysis includes specific steps you can take to improve your network.
  • Free and Accessible Our core analysis tools are free for everyone to use, with no hidden fees or premium paywalls.

Our Technology

WiFi.Report leverages modern web technologies to provide comprehensive network analysis without compromising your privacy:

  • Network Information API Accesses connection type, effective speed, and RTT (Round Trip Time) data directly from your browser.
  • Performance APIs Measures real-world latency and timing information using Navigation Timing and Resource Timing APIs.
  • Security Context Detection Analyzes HTTPS usage, mixed content, and secure context indicators to assess connection security.
  • IP Geolocation Uses third-party APIs to provide location context while respecting privacy boundaries.
  • Local Processing All analysis, calculations, and recommendations are generated client-side using JavaScript.

Our Values

  • Privacy is Fundamental We believe privacy is a right, not a luxury. That's why we never collect, store, or sell user data.
  • Transparency Matters We're open about how our tools work, what data we access, and how we use it. No hidden agendas.
  • Accessibility for All Network security and performance insights should be available to everyone, regardless of technical expertise.
  • Continuous Improvement We're constantly working to improve our analysis algorithms and add new features based on user feedback.
  • User Empowerment We provide information and recommendations, but you're always in control of your network decisions.

Our Team

WiFi.Report is built and maintained by a passionate team of developers, security experts, and user experience designers who care deeply about network privacy and performance.

What's Next

We're constantly working to improve WiFi.Report and add new features. Here's what's on our roadmap:

  • Historical Tracking View your network performance over time with trend analysis (stored locally on your device).
  • Advanced Diagnostics More detailed analysis of DNS performance, packet loss, and connection quality metrics.
  • Router Recommendations Personalized suggestions for router settings and configurations based on your analysis.
  • Mobile Apps Native iOS and Android apps with additional features and offline capabilities.
  • Educational Resources Guides, tutorials, and articles to help you better understand network security and performance.
